Trading Basics

What is Paper Trading?

Paper trading is simulated trading that allows investors to practice buying and selling securities without risking real money. It serves as a learning environment to test investment strategies and familiarize yourself with market dynamics.

Bullish vs Bearish Market

A Bull Market describes an environment where prices are rising, and investor sentiment is positive.

A Bear Market is when prices are falling, and pessimism prevails among investors.

Understanding Market Orders

  • Market Order: Executed immediately at the current market price.
  • Limit Order: Sets a maximum or minimum price at which you are willing to buy or sell.
  • Stop-Loss Order: An order placed to sell a security when it reaches a certain price to limit loss.
